How MeterMate Works

The City of Santa Monica provides detailed information about its metered parking spaces through its free parking data API, including latitude/longitude coordinates, sensor events indicating whether a vehicle has pulled in or pulled out of a metered space, and the date and time such events were recorded.

It's when we integrate this valuable parking data with Google's powerful Google Maps JavaScript API that the real magic happens. Using a RESTful API and a number of cutting-edge JavaScript technologies, we parse through the data, plot the meters' precise locations on the map, and display the information most relevant to you in a user-friendly way.
